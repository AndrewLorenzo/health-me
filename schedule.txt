7-Day Meal Plan
Day 1
Breakfast: Oatmeal with whole milk, banana, honey, peanut butter
Lunch: Grilled chicken breast, brown rice, avocado, mixed vegetables sautéed in olive oil
Dinner: Salmon, mashed sweet potatoes, broccoli with butter
Snack: Trail mix or smoothie (banana + peanut butter + milk + protein powder)

Day 2
Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with cheese and spinach, whole grain toast, full-fat yogurt with berries
Lunch: Beef stir-fry with white rice and mixed vegetables
Dinner: Chicken curry with coconut milk, served with white rice and sautéed green beans
Snack: Cheese and crackers or a granola bar

Day 3
Breakfast: Pancakes with butter, syrup, and a glass of milk
Lunch: Tuna sandwich with mayonnaise, boiled eggs, avocado slices, and fruit
Dinner: Baked chicken thighs, couscous, and roasted carrots
Snack: Greek yogurt with nuts and honey

Day 4
Breakfast: Whole grain cereal with whole milk, a banana, and boiled eggs
Lunch: Pasta with meatballs and tomato sauce, parmesan cheese, side salad with olive oil
Dinner: Grilled steak, baked potatoes with sour cream, sautéed spinach
Snack: Protein smoothie (milk, oats, banana, cocoa powder, peanut butter)

Day 5
Breakfast: French toast with peanut butter, banana slices, and a glass of milk
Lunch: Chicken wrap with hummus, cheese, and veggies; fruit on the side
Dinner: Baked salmon, rice pilaf, roasted asparagus
Snack: Avocado toast or a handful of nuts and dried fruit

Day 6
Breakfast: Omelet with cheese, mushrooms, and avocado, whole grain toast
Lunch: Turkey sandwich with cheese, lettuce, and mayo; potato salad
Dinner: Creamy pasta with shrimp or chicken, garlic bread
Snack: Cottage cheese with fruit or peanut butter on whole grain crackers

Day 7
Breakfast: Smoothie bowl (banana, berries, yogurt, oats, peanut butter), topped with granola
Lunch: Fried rice with egg, chicken, vegetables, sesame oil
Dinner: Lamb stew with potatoes and carrots, served with bread
Snack: Chocolate milk or high-calorie granola bar
