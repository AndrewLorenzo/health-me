About Us

HealthMe is a digital platform designed to help users monitor their daily activities and receive personalized diet recommendations based on their body weight and individual needs.

Our goal is to support a healthier lifestyle through a measured, data-driven approach. With features that are simple yet functional, we are committed to providing an accessible experience for anyone looking to begin or maintain a more balanced way of living. Through our activity tracking and tailored recommendations, we aim to be a reliable companion in your daily decision-making regarding physical activity and nutritional intake.

Thank you for trusting HealthMe as a part of your journey toward a healthier and more organized life.
